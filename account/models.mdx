## Model Specifications

### GPT-4
- **Context Length**: 8,192 tokens
- **Best For**: Complex reasoning, analysis
- **Rate Limit**: 10,000 tokens/minute

### GPT-3.5 Turbo
- **Context Length**: 4,096 tokens  
- **Best For**: General tasks, chat
- **Rate Limit**: 60,000 tokens/minute

### Claude
- **Context Length**: 100,000 tokens
- **Best For**: Long-form content
- **Rate Limit**: 5,000 tokens/minute

## Usage Guidelines

<Tip>

- Use GPT-4 for complex analysis
- Use GPT-3.5 Turbo for general tasks
- Use Claude for long documents
