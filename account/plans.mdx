## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does billing work?">
    Plans are billed monthly or annually. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my limits?">
    On the Free plan, requests are throttled. On paid plans, you'll receive notifications before hitting limits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of the billing period.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
